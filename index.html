Our Story
We’ve been part of the CityCentre community since 2015 — watching it evolve from a shopping destination into a lifestyle hub.

CityCentre attracts an active, health-conscious crowd — people flowing between Lifetime Fitness, Nike, Lululemon, Beyond Yoga, pilates studios, pickleball courts, and run clubs. We saw it every day. And we noticed something missing: there was nowhere to grab clean, quality fuel between workouts, meetings, and errands.

We come from the wellness and fitness industry — running studios, boot camps, pop-ups, and community events. We’re also multi-unit operators with concepts currently running across Houston, so we understand what it takes to execute at scale. This isn’t our first venture — it’s the one we’ve been building toward.

The realization was simple: this neighborhood doesn’t need another coffee shop. It needs a fueling station designed for the pace and values of the people who live here.

Our Vision

Not a coffee shop. A daily ritual for people who move with intention.

The essentials aren’t a restriction — they’re a discipline.
Strip away the noise. What’s left is what matters. Every item on the menu exists because your body needs it. Every surface in the space earns its place. The Collective doesn’t perform minimalism — it practices it. From what we serve to how the space feels, everything unnecessary has been removed.
The menu is designed around how CityCentre moves.
High-protein, high-carb, low-fat fuel for people going from gym to office to errands. Not indulgence for its own sake — nourishment that fits the rhythm. Every ingredient chosen with purpose: egg white paninis, overnight oats, power muffins, cold brew on tap. Food that respects how you spend your day.
Every detail earned its place.
The grab & go station, the panini press, the Collective Cups — all designed for speed without sacrificing substance. Nothing is decorative. Nothing is filler. From the menu board overhead to the bench seating at the window, everything exists because someone will use it, every single day.
The Space

Designed with intention

Warm minimalist concrete aesthetic — concrete counters, arch doorways, ambient lighting, and living plants. Every detail calibrated to feel elevated yet approachable.

The Storefront

Clean signage, dark planters, and a welcoming awning create immediate curb appeal on Town & Country. Outdoor two-tops capture foot traffic from the CityCentre promenade and parking corridor, turning passersby into regulars.

The Interior

Concrete bench seating, a warm counter experience with bar stools, a clean menu board overhead, and a grab-and-go fridge for bottled cold brew and pre-packaged snacks. The arch doorway frames the space and creates an inviting flow.

The Details

1,113 SF of efficient, purpose-built space — 60' of depth delivering a layout that maximizes both guest experience and operational throughput. A flexible footprint ready for morning rush, afternoon remote workers, and evening dessert-and-coffee service.

The Menu

Quality-forward, curated for pace

Grab-and-go focused with paninis as the sole savory offering. Everything else is pick-up-and-go — crafted for speed without sacrificing quality.

Espresso Bar

Lattes, cortados, americanos, single-origin pour-overs, and seasonal specials. The foundation of our program — every shot pulled with precision.

Signature Drinks

Matcha lattes, functional beverages, cold brews, and specialty iced drinks. Designed to photograph as well as they taste.

Pastries & Bakery

Croissants, danishes, protein muffins, banana oat bars, and cookies — rotating daily from local bakery partners. Fresh every morning, with macro-conscious options built into the rotation.

Paninis

Our signature savory — pressed sandwiches with premium ingredients, sealed into a clean, portable shape. No mess, no falling ingredients. A warm, satisfying meal designed for people on the move. Breakfast paninis for the morning crowd — egg white, turkey, avocado — and savory builds for lunch.

Why CityCentre

Proven demand. We elevate the experience.

  • Premium coffee demand at this location is well-established — the category has consistently performed here
  • CityCentre’s tenant mix has no specialty café offering quality-forward coffee paired with health-conscious food
  • The wellness ecosystem — Lifetime Fitness, Beyond Yoga, Lululemon, Nike, pilates studios, run clubs — creates daily demand for clean fuel with nowhere to find it
  • The Collective fills the white space between casual chain coffee and full-service dining

CityCentre's community is our community.

  • Same demographic as Lululemon, Alo, Beyond Yoga, Nike, and Lifetime Fitness
  • Health-conscious, active, affluent professionals — the exact customer who seeks out specialty coffee
  • Remote workers seeking a "third place" between home and office
  • After-dinner dessert + coffee crowd currently underserved in the tenant mix
  • 4.6M annual visitors, 247K daytime population, 2nd highest-earning zip code in Houston

More than a café — a community anchor

Coffee. Culture. Collective.

More than a tagline — it's the ethos behind everything we do. The Collective is built to be a gathering point for the CityCentre community: a place where morning routines, afternoon work sessions, and evening connections all feel intentional.

From run clubs on the green to seasonal activations and local artist features, we're designing a café that gives back to the neighborhood that fuels it.

Run Club

Morning runs from The Collective, ending with post-run coffee on the CityCentre green. Drives consistent morning foot traffic and builds brand loyalty with the active community.

Wellness Events

Pilates sessions and yoga pop-ups on the CityCentre green space. Cross-promote with Beyond Yoga, Lululemon, and Lifetime Fitness — driving traffic to neighboring tenants, not just to us.

Seasonal Activations

Holiday markets, latte art competitions, local artist features. Align with CityCentre's 300+ annual events to amplify foot traffic for the entire property.
