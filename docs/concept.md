# The Collective — Brand Concept

## What It Is
A specialty coffee cafe + pastries + grab-and-go + paninis in CityCentre Houston (former Starbucks space, 1,113 SF on Town & Country Blvd).

## Tagline
**Coffee. Culture. Collective.**

## Positioning
"Not a coffee shop. A daily ritual for people who move with intention."

## Concept Statement
A warm minimalist cafe built around quality-forward beverages, curated pastries, and community connection — designed to serve the morning-to-evening rhythm of CityCentre.

## The Four Pillars
1. **Specialty Coffee** — Third-wave espresso program with single-origin and seasonal blends
2. **Curated Pastries** — Rotating selection of croissants, danishes, and baked goods from local partners
3. **Grab & Go** — Designed for the CityCentre pace — quick, elevated, effortless
4. **Community Hub** — Run clubs, wellness events, and activations on the CityCentre green

## Design Aesthetic
- **Style**: Warm minimalist — health & wellness focused
- **Materials**: Concrete counters, arch doorways, ambient lighting, living plants, concrete bench seating
- **Philosophy**: Remove everything unnecessary. Every item on the menu exists because your body needs it. Every surface in the space earns its place. The identity is about essentials, discipline, and fueling active lives — not about concrete as an aesthetic trend.
