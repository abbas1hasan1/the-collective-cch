# The Collective — The Space

## Location
Former Starbucks, Town & Country Blvd, CityCentre Houston

## Size
1,113 SF of efficient, purpose-built space with 60' of depth maximizing guest experience and operational throughput.

## Three Components

### 1. The Storefront
Clean signage, dark planters, welcoming awning on Town & Country. Outdoor two-tops capture CityCentre promenade and parking corridor foot traffic.

### 2. The Interior
- Concrete bench seating
- Warm counter with bar stools
- Clean overhead menu board
- Grab-and-go fridge for bottled cold brew and pre-packaged snacks
- Arch doorway frames space and creates inviting flow

### 3. The Details
1,113 SF efficient layout ready for:
- Morning rush
- Afternoon remote workers
- Evening dessert-and-coffee service
