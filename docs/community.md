# The Collective — Community Vision

## Ethos
"More than a cafe — a community anchor. Coffee. Culture. Collective."

## Core Activities

### 1. Run Club
Weekly Saturday morning runs from The Collective, ending with post-run coffee on CityCentre green. Drives consistent morning foot traffic and builds brand loyalty with the active community.

### 2. Wellness Events
Pilates sessions and yoga pop-ups on CityCentre green. Cross-promote with Beyond Yoga, Lululemon, Lifetime Fitness — driving traffic to neighboring tenants.

### 3. Seasonal Activations
Holiday markets, latte art competitions, local artist features. Align with CityCentre's 300+ annual events to amplify foot traffic for the entire property.

## Why This Matters for the Landlord
- Drives foot traffic to neighboring tenants through cross-promotion
- Aligns with CityCentre's existing 300+ event calendar
- Creates a community anchor that strengthens the overall tenant ecosystem
- Fills a gap that the active, wellness-oriented customer base is actively looking for
