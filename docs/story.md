# The Collective — Our Story

## Founders
- **Abbas Hasan** — Tech, design, and presentation lead
- **Ali Asad Hasan** — Restaurant and coffee operator (Sugar Land cafe experience)

## Background
- Own Garden Montessori Schools (5 campuses, 6th opening Summer 2026)
- Multi-unit operations expertise across education, retail, and F&B
- 10-year CityCentre community members (since 2015)
- Abbas ran a fitness studio and boot camp — understands the active lifestyle from the inside

## The Insight
"This neighborhood doesn't need another coffee shop. It needs a fueling station designed for the pace and values of the people who live here."

## Why Them
- CityCentre attracts an active, health-conscious crowd flowing between Lifetime Fitness, Nike, Lululemon, Beyond Yoga, pilates studios, pickleball courts, run clubs
- Abbas and Ali identified the gap: nowhere to grab clean, quality fuel between workouts, meetings, and errands
- They understand the community because they are the community

## Our Vision (The Philosophy)
1. **Concrete Aesthetic as Ethos** — Not a trend but a reflection of values. Stripped back, honest, built to last.
2. **Menu Designed Around Movement** — Nourishment with purpose, not indulgence for its own sake.
3. **Every Detail Earned Its Place** — Grab & go station, panini press, Collective Cups. Designed for speed without sacrificing substance.
