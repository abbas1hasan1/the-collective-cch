The Concept
COFFEE.  CULTURE.  COLLECTIVE.

Specialty coffee, warm bites, and protein-rich food with real carbs and substance, designed for on the go. A space built around what matters. Stone surfaces, natural light, and nothing unnecessary. From morning espresso to an evening reset, every detail serves the rhythm of your day.

Every detail earns its place.

Warm light, natural textures, and open sight lines that let the space breathe. An arch doorway frames the room. A clean menu board sits overhead. Every surface is honest, every material grounded. Nothing extra, nothing competing for your attention. Just a space that moves with your day.

The Space

Designed with intention

Warm Minimalism
Every material earned its place — nothing decorative, nothing excess. Warm lighting brings out the stone textures at night.
Matte Finishes
Matte surfaces reduce light reflections, creating a calmer, less distracting environment. The space feels grounded, not glossy.
Indoor-Outdoor Flow
An open-door layout invites natural light and foot traffic. Inside and outside dissolve into one — easier to walk in, easier to linger.
Concrete Continuity
The CityCentre promenade material continues inside. No hard line between out there and in here — one flow.
The Menu

Quality-forward beverages, grab-and-go nourishment, and warm bites — designed for the morning-to-evening rhythm of CityCentre.

Drinks

The full beverage program — from espresso to cold brew on tap

Espresso is the foundation — lattes, cortados, pour-overs — alongside signature drinks like matcha lattes and functional beverages. Cold brew on tap means zero friction: low-acid, high-caffeine, available in branded cans that extend the brand into CityCentre.

Snacks

Grab-and-go nourishment, anchored by Collective Cups

The signature concept — you literally collect your cup. Fresh fruit, vegetables, nuts, protein combos portioned to bridge workouts and meetings. Oatmeals, overnight oats, power muffins, and rotating local pastries round out the nourishment station.

Paninis

The only made-to-order item, sealed for the move

Pressed format is deliberate — seals ingredients in, clean portable shape, no mess. Egg white, turkey, avocado mornings; savory builds for lunch. One warm made-to-order item that keeps the line moving while everything else stays grab-and-go.

Our Story
We’ve been part of the CityCentre community since 2015, watching it evolve into the lifestyle hub it is today.

CityCentre attracts an active, health-conscious crowd. People flowing between Lifetime Fitness, Nike, Lululemon, Beyond Yoga, pilates studios, pickleball courts, and run clubs. We saw it every day. And we noticed something missing: there was nowhere to grab clean, quality nourishment between workouts, meetings, and errands.

We come from the wellness and fitness industry. Running studios, boot camps, pop-ups, and community events. We’re also multi-unit operators with concepts currently running across Houston, so we understand what it takes to execute at scale. This isn’t our first venture. It’s the one we’ve been building toward.

The realization was simple: this neighborhood is ready for something new. A nourishment station designed for the pace and values of the people who live here.
