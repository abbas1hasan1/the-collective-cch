# The Collective — Why CityCentre

## Market Stats
- **4.6M** annual visitors
- **247K** daytime population
- **2nd highest-earning zip code** in Houston
- **300+** annual events on the CityCentre green
- 50-acre mixed-use development (Beltway 8 & I-10)

## Market Demand
- Premium coffee demand well-established (Starbucks validated before closing nationally)
- **White space**: No specialty cafe offering quality-forward coffee paired with health-conscious food
- Wellness ecosystem creates daily demand for clean fuel with nowhere to find it
- Fills gap between casual chain coffee and full-service dining

## Target Customer
Same demographic as Lululemon, Alo, Beyond Yoga, Nike, Lifetime Fitness:
- Health-conscious, active, affluent professionals
- Specialty coffee seekers
- Remote workers seeking "third place" between home and office
- After-dinner dessert + coffee crowd (currently underserved)

## Tenant Ecosystem
Lululemon, Nike, Beyond Yoga, Bella Green, Jeni's, Sephora, Anthropologie, Lifetime Fitness

## Competitive Context
- **Joe & The Juice** coming to CityCentre 6 — different audience (fast-casual juice vs. specialty coffee)
- No direct specialty coffee competitor in the development
